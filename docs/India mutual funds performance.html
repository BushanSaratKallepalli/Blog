Mutual funds

Key points

1. For tax saving it is clearly Mirae Asset Tax Saver Fund

2. Mostly these funds invest in stable and large companies

3. Mutual fund investments are generally for > 3ys

4. In India for next 10 yrs returns on mutual funds should beat Index returns( Nifty, Nifty midcap etc) by 2 to 5%

5. For people who are looking for Bank like returns look into HDFC Corporate Bond Fund - Growth which will generally give you 8% stable returns ( Pre- tax)

6. Index funds are best suited for people who want safe returns and need money (some times within one month) in short duration - IDFC Nifty Fund

7. All mutual funds amount can be withdrawn within 2 days except ELSS for tax reasons

8. Do not get carried by ADs on mutual funds, there will be years when you get negative returns

9. ELSS is OK option and not the best option. You can always think on investing 50% in ELSS and rest as bulk, even if you do not follow stocks ( when market sentiment is low.. opposite to now)

10. For people who want to invest more than 30 lakhs and wait for 5 yrs PMS options are best

11. Still for long term in India real estate may beat stock returns (due diligence of property is key decider of returns)
