Reasons on housing

  • Political parties in India are providing free housing plots. Next stop is Construction of houses
  • India is land of free time and scarcity of money
  • Finance to middle and low income groups is tough forcing them to build houses with earned income
  • Construction workers are tough to manage
  • Indians always crave for Own house as against rented house.
  • Building 10 lakh apartment ( the right ticket for low and middle class) in tier 3 towns is like building One lakh Nano. It needs strong political will or Reliance Jio like pricing
  • Housing will remain unorganized sector In India for next 5-30 yrs as profit expectations of builders are high, finance costs and material costs are high coupled with low land cost compared to construction costs
  • Sector was in secular bull market from 2005 to 2022. Hope to continue so ( previous portfolio)
